# Live-Commerce Compliance Blacklist

This reference is a starter list. Treat it as configurable and update it with current platform and legal policy.

## Extreme Words

- 最、第一、顶级、国家级、全网最低、永久、彻底、绝对、100%、零风险、保证、无敌、唯一、首选、必买。

## High-Risk Claim Types

- Medical treatment, prevention, cure, rehabilitation, slimming treatment, disease claims.
- Health supplement efficacy without approved wording.
- Financial returns, investment safety, income guarantee.
- False origin, certification, patent, inspection, celebrity endorsement.
- False scarcity, false countdown, false stock, false original price.

## Safe Handling

- Replace absolutes with verifiable comparative or experiential language.
- Mark price, stock, ranking, sales volume, and effect claims as `需用户确认` unless provided.
- Refuse to generate prohibited medical, financial, or deceptive claims.
